Content Updated Within 3 Months Earns 54% More AI Citations

Pages updated within the last 3 months average 6.0 AI citations, compared to 3.9 citations for content older than 2 years — a 54% advantage. AI assistants also cite content that is 25.7% fresher than traditional organic search results. Based on SE Ranking's analysis of 2.3 million pages across 295,000 domains (November 2025) and Ahrefs' study of 17 million citations (2025).

What this means: AI engines have a strong freshness bias. Regularly updating existing content is more effective than publishing new pages infrequently. For Voyage customers, this means implementing a content refresh cadence — updating key pages with new data points and current dates every 1-3 months — directly increases the likelihood of being cited by ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity.
