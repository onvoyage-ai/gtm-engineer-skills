ChatGPT Drives 87.4% of All AI Referral Traffic

ChatGPT accounts for 87.4% of all AI-driven referral traffic to websites, dwarfing Perplexity (6.2%), Gemini (3.1%), Claude (2.1%), and others combined (1.2%). Based on Conductor's analysis of 13,700 domains and 3.3 billion sessions through November 2025. This concentration means optimizing for ChatGPT citations delivers the most immediate traffic impact.

What this means: ChatGPT is not just the most popular AI assistant — it is the dominant source of AI-driven website traffic by a wide margin. For brands investing in GEO, optimizing content for ChatGPT citation patterns (answer-first structure, quotable blocks, comparison tables) delivers the highest ROI. However, Perplexity's 6.2% share is notable given its smaller user base, suggesting Perplexity users click through to sources at a higher rate.
